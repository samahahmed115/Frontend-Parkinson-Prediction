What is Parkinson's Disease?

Parkinson's Disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement. It starts with mild symptoms such as tremors and muscle stiffness, and can progress to issues with balance and walking.

As the disease advances, individuals may experience difficulty with speech, writing, and other motor functions. While there is no cure, treatments and lifestyle adjustments can help manage symptoms and improve quality of life.

Symptoms of the Disease

  • ✔ Tremors in the hands, arms, or legs
  • ✔ Muscle stiffness
  • ✔ Slowness of movement
  • ✔ Balance problems
